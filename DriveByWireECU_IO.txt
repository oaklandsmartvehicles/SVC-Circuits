DriveByWire ECU I/O

Steering System:
	Inputs:
		Analog: 	SteeringPosition 	//ADC feedback from the linear potentiometer steering sensor
		
	Ouputs:
		Digital: 	SteeringEnable 		//Relay for power on/off
		Digital: 	SteeringDirection 	//Transistor to control left/right relay
		PWM: 							//PWM output to control power
		
Acceleration:
	Inputs:
		Digital (Interupt): WheelSpeedLeft //Left Front Wheel Speed
		Digital (Interupt): WheelSpeedRight //Right Front Wheel Speed
		
	Ouputs:
		PWM: 		Acceleration Pedal Position
		Digital: 	Acceleration Engaged
		Digital:	Reverse relay signal
		Digital:	Vehicle reverse control reconnect relay
		
Braking:
	Inputs:
		N/A
		
	Ouputs:
		PWM:		Braking Force
		
Safety Lights:
	Inputs:
		N/A
	
	Outputs:
		Digital:	Relay for Light 1
		Digital:	Relay for Light 2
		
Emergency Stop:
	Inputs:
		Digital: 	EStopState
		
Indications:
	Inputs:
		N/A
		
	Outputs:
		Digital:	PC Comm
		Digital:	EStop State
		Digital: 	LED 1
		Digital:	LED 2
